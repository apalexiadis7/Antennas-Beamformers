
This work is about the design and analysis of beamforming systems; an antenna array of seven elements and an optical phased array.
The final PDF report is included, alongside MATLAB codes and an image of an optical phased array.
This work was assigned for the course of Special Antennas at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki by Dimitra Ketzaki and Traianos Gioultsis.  
The matlab files: 
beamformer_initial.m: the case of seven antennas with separation d=λ/2  
beamformer1.m: the case of seven antennas with separation d=λ/4  
beamformer2.m: the case of nine antennas with separation d=λ/2  
beamformer3.m: the case of five antennas with separation d=λ/2
